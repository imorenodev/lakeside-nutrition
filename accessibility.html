Accessibility Statement

Our Commitment

Lakeside Nutrition is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply the relevant accessibility standards to ensure we provide equal access to all users.

Conformance Status

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2, Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, language, learning, and neurological disabilities.

Measures Taken

Lakeside Nutrition takes the following measures to ensure accessibility of our website:

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy
  • Descriptive alternative text for all meaningful images
  • Full keyboard navigation support with visible focus indicators
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios meeting WCAG 2.2 AA thresholds
  • Mobile-responsive design with accessible navigation
  • Menu content provided as real HTML text readable by screen readers
  • Respect for user motion preferences via prefers-reduced-motion
  • Skip-to-content link for keyboard and screen reader users
  • ARIA attributes where necessary to enhance assistive technology support

Compatibility

This website is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen readers (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver, TalkBack)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Speech recognition software
  • Keyboard-only navigation

Third-Party Content

Our website may include embedded content from third parties (such as Instagram posts) that may not be fully within our control. We encourage users who experience difficulty with third-party content to contact us directly so we can provide the information in an alternative format.
